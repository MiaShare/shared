Engineering Notes

Datamart Rebuild Skip Windows

All reportbuilder:* and contracts:backfill-* commands skip execution during two critical windows:

  • Backup: 05:00–06:59 UTC (21:00–22:59 PT PDT)
  • Payment Processing: 00:00–01:00 PT (midnight window)

Payment Processing Sequence

Automatic payments run sequentially starting at midnight PT with 10-15 min staggering and withoutOverlapping(30) + onOneServer():

  1. 00:00 — Interest accruals
  2. 00:15 — ISA payments
  3. 00:20 — Installment payments
  4. 00:30 — Retail installment payments
  5. 02:15 — Payment promises
  6. 07:00 — Reattempt failed payments
